  • Assignment 3.2

    Assets

    The branding for Master was not yet as complete as it became in the coming stages. I was still playing with colors at this point, I tried having a secondary colors but it was too distracting so I returned to the shades of blue. The typeface "Nunito", was chosen because it most emodies the fun and light feeling that I wanted Master to evoke. The rounded corners on all the buttons and boxs also contributed to the youthful aesthetic.
